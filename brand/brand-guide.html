06 — Voice

Write like a human.

Confident without being cocky. Smart without being nerdy. Write like you're texting a friend who runs a business.

Do
  • Short, punchy sentences
  • Lead with the benefit
  • Be specific — use numbers
  • Australian English (colour, optimise)
  • Say "you" and "your"
Don't
  • Corporate jargon ("leverage", "synergy")
  • Over-explain — trust the reader
  • Be vague ("improve your marketing")
  • Passive voice when active works
  • Say "AI-powered" in every sentence
✓ This

"Your website visitors are already asking questions. Convo turns those conversations into blog posts that rank."

✗ Not this

"Our AI-powered solution leverages natural language processing to facilitate automated content generation from conversational inputs."
